About me

After graduating as an advertising graphic designer, the need to
specialize led me to look for new stimuli, always remaining in the
graphic and technological field.

After evaluating several options, my attention has been captured by
the Start2Impact platform, in particular by the UX/UI Design course.

I would describe myself as a particularly empathetic person, the idea
of ​​combining psychology, user research and graphics in some way
particularly appealed to me.
